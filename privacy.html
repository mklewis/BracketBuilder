Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Information the Add-on Accesses

PATS Bracket Builder accesses information in the Google Sheets™ spreadsheet from which the user runs the add-on. This may include participant names, contact information, divisions, event selections, ratings, and other tournament registration information entered by the user or collected through Google Forms™.

2. How Information Is Used

The add-on uses spreadsheet information only to organize tournament entries and create bracket workbooks and related files in the user's Google Drive™.

3. Data Storage and Transmission

PATS Bracket Builder does not operate an external database and does not transmit tournament registration data to servers controlled by the developer. Processing occurs through Google Apps Script and Google Workspace™ services within the user's Google account.

4. Data Sharing and Sale

The developer does not sell user data. The add-on does not share user data with advertisers, analytics providers, or unrelated third parties.

5. Developer Access

The developer does not independently browse or retrieve a user's spreadsheets or Google Drive™ files. The add-on acts only when an authorized user runs it and only within the permissions granted by that user.

6. Data Security

The add-on relies on Google account authorization and Google Workspace™ security controls. Users are responsible for managing sharing settings and access to their spreadsheets and generated files.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

The developer does not retain copies of user spreadsheet data. Users may delete generated bracket files from Google Drive™ and may revoke the add-on's access through their Google Account security settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The add-on is intended for use by tournament organizers and other adults. It is not directed to children under 13. Tournament organizers are responsible for collecting and handling participant information in accordance with applicable law and organizational policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the most recent revision.
