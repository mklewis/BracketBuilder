Getting Started with PATS Bracket Builder

Last updated: July 13, 2026

PATS Bracket Builder creates tournament bracket workbooks from a Google Sheets™ registration response sheet.

What the add-on creates:

When you run the add-on, it creates a new spreadsheet in your Google Drive™. The new spreadsheet contains a separate tab for each singles or doubles event found in the source registration sheet.

Source Spreadsheet Format

Your source spreadsheet should contain one row per player and use the column headings shown below. The headings should remain in the first row.

Important Formatting Notes

Use the same event names consistently for all players in the same division. For example, use 12&Under Boys Singles for every player in that event rather than mixing different spellings or abbreviations.

UTR values may be entered as a number such as 1.35 or as a range such as 1-3. Doubles partner names should be entered when a player has a confirmed partner.

Do not remove the header row. Additional unused columns may be present, but the required headings above should remain unchanged.

Generate Brackets

Open the completed registration spreadsheet and choose:

Extensions > PATS Bracket Builder > Generate Brackets

After processing is complete, a new bracket spreadsheet will be created in your Google Drive™. Each event will appear on its own tab.
