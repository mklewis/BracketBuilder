What to Include

To help us troubleshoot, please include a description of the issue, the exact error message, a screenshot if available, and the column headings from the spreadsheet being used. Do not email sensitive participant information unless it is necessary to resolve the issue.

Basic Troubleshooting

Confirm that you are signed into the Google account that installed the add-on, refresh the spreadsheet, and then open Extensions > PATS Bracket Builder. Also confirm that your registration sheet contains the expected column headings.
