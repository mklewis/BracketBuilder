Developer Information

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Michael Lewis

Michael Lewis is the independent developer and maintainer of PATS Bracket Builder. He is a software developer based in Amarillo, Texas, and created the add-on to help tennis tournament organizers automate bracket generation from registration spreadsheets.

About PATS Bracket Builder

PATS Bracket Builder works with Google Sheets™ to organize tournament entries and generate bracket workbooks in Google Drive™.
